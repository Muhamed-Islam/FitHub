Diet Plans

Muscle Fuel Masterplan

  • Breakfast: Protein-rich smoothie with Greek yogurt, mixed berries, spinach, and protein powder
  • Snack: Almonds and a piece of fruit (e.g., apple or banana).
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken breast with quinoa, steamed broccoli, and a side salad with olive oil dressing.
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with sweet potato, roasted asparagus, and a green salad with avocado.

Performance Powerhouse Plan

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal topped with sliced almonds, Greek yogurt, and berries.
  • Snack: Apple slices with peanut butter.
  • Lunch: Grilled salmon with quinoa salad (quinoa, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, and feta cheese).
  • Dinner Baked chicken breast with sweet potato wedges, steamed green beans, and a side of mixed greens with balsamic vinaigrette.:

Power Protein Protocol

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with whole grain toast and avocado.
  • Snack: Protein shake made with whey protein powder, almond milk, and a banana.
  • Lunch: Turkey and avocado wrap with whole grain tortilla, mixed greens, and hummus.
  • Dinner: Lean beef stir-fry with brown rice, mixed vegetables, and teriyaki sauce.

Strength & Stamina Strategy

  • Breakfast: Whole grain toast with scrambled eggs, spinach, and feta cheese.
  • Snack: Trail mix with nuts, seeds, and dried fruit.
  • Lunch: Turkey and avocado sandwich on whole grain bread with lettuce, tomato, and mustard.
  • Dinner: Baked cod with wild rice, steamed broccoli, and a side salad with vinaigrette dressing.
